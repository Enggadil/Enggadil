## Who I Am

I'm the **founder of Ranea.ai**, where I build AI systems that solve real problems in healthcare and human performance. My work sits at the intersection of **LLM engineering, mobile development, and clinical research** — shipping products used by real patients, not just demos.

Currently leading the engineering and research behind **mHealth**, an AI-powered cognitive rehabilitation platform for cancer patients, built in collaboration with university research teams.
